Founded in 1965, our university started as a small institution with a mission to provide high-quality education to students from all backgrounds. Over the decades, we have transformed into a world-class research and academic institution , shaping some of the brightest minds across the globe.

Our journey has been defined by continuous progress, driven by our faculty, students, and alumni who have made remarkable contributions to science, technology, business, and society. Today, we stand as a beacon of knowledge, preparing students for a rapidly evolving world.
